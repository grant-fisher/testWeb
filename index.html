About Kimbia Athletics

KIMbia represents some of the best distance runners in the world. Our athletes compete at the highest level of the sport, winning Major Marathons and National Championships, and taking home medals at the World Championships and Olympic Games. We believe a sport exists outside the arena, wherever it is watched, discussed, and pursued. Our goal, as always, is to bring athletes and fans together in a more perfect union.. and bring athletics to life.
